Mission and goals


The Sustainable Development Goals were adopted by the United Nations in 2015 as a universal call to action to end poverty, protect the planet, and ensure that by 2030 all people enjoy peace and prosperity.

When specifically looking at Life Below Water our aims are:

Marine Pollution

By 2025, prevent and significantly reduce marine pollution of all kinds, in particular from land-based activities, including marine debris and nutrient pollution.

Resourceful fishing

By 2030, increase the economic benefits to Small Island developing States and least developed countries from the sustainable use of marine resources.

Changes to the law

Enhance the conservation and sustainable use of oceans and their resources by implementing international law as reflected in United Nations Convention on the Law of the Sea.
